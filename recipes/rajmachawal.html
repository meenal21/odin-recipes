Rajma Chawal

Description

Rajma Chawal served hot with ghee

Using freshly boiled fried jeera rice with rajma gravy along its side gives out a flavorful aroma giving a tingling sensation of watering mouth.
Writing this description has made me redo my dinner plans for tomorrow for Rajma Chawal!

Ingredients:

  • Rice
  • Rajma
  • Onions
  • Tomatoes
  • Ginger and Garlic

Steps

  1. Boil Rice and then fry it in ghee with jeera
  2. Boil Rajma under soft
  3. Make tomato puree and fry it with onions and ginger garlic along with some chilli in vegetable oil
  4. Serve Hot!
